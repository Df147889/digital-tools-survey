Internal Survey

Daily Digital Tools Usage Survey

This survey aims to understand which digital tools employees use in their daily work. The results will help improve systems, enhance efficiency, and support better digital solutions across the organization.

What do we mean by "Digital Tools"?

Digital tools are any systems or online platforms you use regularly to perform your job tasks, manage your work, or communicate with your team.

A tool must meet these conditions to be included:

  • It is related to your work tasks
  • It helps you complete your job or manage your responsibilities
  • You use it regularly (daily or almost daily)

Include

  • HR systems (e.g., Zoho People)
  • CRM systems
  • Timesheet / time tracking systems
  • Project management tools (e.g., Jira, ClickUp)
  • Communication tools (e.g., Teams, Slack)

Do Not Include

  • General office programs (Word, PowerPoint)
  • Personal tools unrelated to your job
  • Tools you rarely use
  • Tools that don't impact your daily work

Employee Information

Digital Tools

Please list all the digital tools you use in your daily work. For each tool, provide the name, how you use it, and how often.

Important Notes

  • Only include tools you actually use — not tools you have access to but don't use
  • Be as accurate and clear as possible
  • If you're unsure whether a tool should be included, add it anyway and describe how you use it
